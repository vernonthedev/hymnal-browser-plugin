[Slide 1]
1. Be still, my soul: the Lord is on thy side; Bear patiently the cross of grief or pain; Leave to thy God to order and provide; In every change He faithful will remain.
[Slide 2]
Be Still, my soul: thy best, thy heavenly friend Through thorny ways leads to a joyful end.
[Slide 3]
2. Be still, my soul: thy God doth undertake To guide the future as He has the past. Thy hope, thy confidence let nothing shake; All now mysterious shall be bright at last
[Slide 4]
Be Still, my soul: The waves and winds still know His voice who ruled them while He dwelt below.
[Slide 5]
3. Be still, my soul: the hour is hastening on When we shall be forever with the Lord, When disappointment, grief, and fear are gone, Sorrow forgot, love’s purest joys restored.
[Slide 6]
Be Still, my soul: When change and tears are past, All safe and blessed we shall meet at last
