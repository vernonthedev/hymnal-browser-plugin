[Slide 1]
1. Jesus calls us o'er the tumult Of our life's wild, restless sea; Day by day his sweet voice soundeth, Saying, "Christian, follow Me.“
[Slide 2]
2. Jesus calls us from the worship Of the vain world's golden store, From each idol that would keep us, Saying, “Christian, love Me more”.
[Slide 3]
3. In our joys and in our sorrows, Days of toil and hours of ease, Still He calls, in cares and pleasures, "Christian, love Me more than these."
[Slide 4]
4. Jesus calls us! By thy mercies, Savior, may we hear Thy call, Give our hearts to Thy obedience, Serve and love Thee best of all.
