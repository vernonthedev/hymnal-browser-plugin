[Slide 1]
1. Alleluia, alleluia, alleluia! The strife is o'er, the battle done; Now is the victor's triumph won! Now be the song of praise begun. Alleluia!
[Slide 2]
2. The pow'rs of death have done their worst, But Christ their legions has dispersed. Let shouts of holy joy outburst. Alleluia!
[Slide 3]
3. The three sad days have quickly sped, He rises glorious from the dead. All glory to our risen head! Alleluia!
[Slide 4]
4. Lord, by the stripes which wounded You From death's sting free Your servants too, That we may live and sing to You. Alleluia! Alleluia, alleluia, alleluia!
