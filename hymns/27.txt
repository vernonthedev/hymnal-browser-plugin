1. Rejoice, ye pure in heart! Rejoice, give thanks, and sing; Your festal banner wave on high, The cross of Christ your King. Rejoice! Rejoice! Rejoice, give thanks, and sing!
2. With voice as full and strong As ocean’s surging praise, Send forth the sturdy hymns of old, The psalms of ancient days Rejoice! Rejoice! Rejoice, give thanks, and sing!
3. With all the angel choirs, With all the saints on earth Pour out the strains of joy and bliss, True rapture, noblest mirth. Rejoice! Rejoice! Rejoice, give thanks, and sing!
4. Yes, on through life’s long path, Still chanting as ye go; From youth to age, by night and day, In gladness and in woe. Rejoice! Rejoice! Rejoice, give thanks, and sing!
5. Praise Him who reigns on high, The Lord whom we adore The Father, Son and Holy Ghost, One God forevermore. Rejoice! Rejoice! Rejoice, give thanks, and sing!
