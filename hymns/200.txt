[Slide 1]
1. The Lord is coming, let this be The herald note of jubilee; And when we meet and when we part, The salutation from the heart. The Lord is coming, let this be The herald note of jubilee; The herald note of jubilee.
[Slide 2]
2. The Lord is coming! sound it forth From east to west, from south to north; Speed on! speed on the tidings glad, That none who love Him may be sad. The Lord is coming, let this be The herald note of jubilee; The herald note of jubilee.
[Slide 3]
3. The Lord is coming, swift and sure And all His judgments shall endure, And none can hope to escape His wrath, Who walk not in the narrow path. The Lord is coming, let this be The herald note of jubilee; The herald note of jubilee.
[Slide 4]
4. This earth, with her ten thousand wrongs Will soon be tuned to noblest songs; Our praise shall then, in realms of light, With all His universe unite. The Lord is coming, let this be The herald note of jubilee; The herald note of jubilee.
