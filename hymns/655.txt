[Slide 1]
1. Happy the home when God is there, And love fills every breast; When one their wish, and one their prayer, And one their heavenly rest.
[Slide 2]
2. Happy the home where Jesus' name Is sweet to every ear; Where children early speak His fame, And parents hold Him dear.
[Slide 3]
3.Happy the home where prayer is heard, And praise is wont to rise; Where parents love the Sacred Word And all its wisdom prize.
[Slide 4]
4.  Lord, let us in our homes agree This blessed peace to gain; Unite our hearts in love to Thee, And love to all will reign.
