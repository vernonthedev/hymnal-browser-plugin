[Slide 1]
1. Lord, I have made Thy Word my choice, My lasting heritage; There shall my noblest pow’rs rejoice, My warmest thoughts engage.
[Slide 2]
2. I’ll read the histories of Thy love, And keep the laws insight; While through Thy promises I rove, With ever fresh delight.
[Slide 3]
3. In this broad land of wealth unknown, Where springs of life arise, Seeds of immortal bliss are sown, And hidden glory lies.
[Slide 4]
4. The best relief that mourners have, It makes our sorrows blest; Our fairest hope beyond the grave, And our eternal rest.
