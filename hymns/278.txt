[Slide 1]
1. Lord Jesus, once You spoke to men Upon the mountain and the plain, O help us listen now as then And wonder at Your words again.
[Slide 2]
2. We all have secret fears to face, Our minds and motives to amend. We seek Your truth, we need Your grace, Our Living Lord and present friend.
[Slide 3]
3. The gospel speaks; and we receive Your light, Your love, Your own command. O help us live what we believe, In daily word of heart and hand.
