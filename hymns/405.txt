[Slide 1]
1. O God, unseen, yet ever near, Reveal Thy presence now While we in love that hath no fear, Before Thy glory bow.
[Slide 2]
2. Here may Thy faithful people know The blessings of Thy love, The streams that thro’ the dessert flow, The manna from above.
[Slide 3]
3. We come, obedient to Thy Word To feast on heav’nly food, Our meat, the body of our Lord, Our drink, His precious blood
[Slide 4]
4. Thus may we all Thy words obey, For we, O God, are Thine, And go rejoicing on our way, Renewed by strength divine.
