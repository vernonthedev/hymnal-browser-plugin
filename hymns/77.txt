[Slide 1]
1. O love of God most full, O love of God most free, Come warm my heart, come fill my soul, Come lead me unto Thee.
[Slide 2]
2. Warm as the glowing sun So shines Thy love on me, It wraps me ‘round with kindly care, It draws me unto Thee.
[Slide 3]
3. The wildest sea is calm, The tempest brings no fear, The darkest night is full of light, Because Thy love is near.
[Slide 4]
4. O love of God most full, O love of God most free, It warms my heart, it fills my soul, With might it strengthens me.
