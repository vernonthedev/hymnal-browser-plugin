Out of the depths I have cried to you, O Lord; Lord, hear my voice! Let Your ears be attentive. To the voice of my supplication.
If You, Lord, should mark iniquities, O Lord, who would stand? But there is forgiveness with you, That You may be feared I wait for the Lord, my soul waits, And in His word I do hope.
My soul wait for the Lord, More than those who watch for the morning- I say, more than those who watch for the morning.
O Israel, hope in the Lord; For with the Lord there is mercy, And with Him is abundant redemption. And He shall redeem Israel, From all his iniquities.
