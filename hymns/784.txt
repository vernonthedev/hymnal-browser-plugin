[Slide 1]
Blessed is the man who walks not in the counsel of the wicked, nor stands in the way of sinners, nor sits in the seat of scoffers;
[Slide 2]
but his delight is in the lawbof the Lord, and on  his law he meditate day and night.
[Slide 3]
He is like a tree planted by streams of water, that yields its fruit its season, and its leaf does not wither. In all that he does, he prospers.
[Slide 4]
The wicked are not so, but are like chaff which the wind drives away.
[Slide 5]
Therefore the will not stand in the judgment, nor sinners in the congregation of the righteous;
[Slide 6]
For the Lord knows the way of the righteous, but the way of the wickedbwill perish.
