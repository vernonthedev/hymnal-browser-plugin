[Slide 1]
1. Spirit divine, attend our prayers, And make this house Thy home; Descend with all Thy gracious powers, O come, great Spirit, come!
[Slide 2]
2. Come as the light; to us reveal Our emptiness and woe, And lead us in those paths of life Whereon the righteous go.
[Slide 3]
3. Come as the fire, and purge our hearts Like sacrificial flame; Let our whole soul an off’ring be To our Redeemer’s name.
[Slide 4]
4. Come as the dove, And spread Thy wings, The wings of peaceful love; And let Thy church earth become Blest as the church above.
[Slide 5]
5. Spirit divine, attend our prayers, Make a lost world Thy home; Descend with all Thy gracious powers, O come, great Spirit, come!
