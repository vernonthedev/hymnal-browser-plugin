[Slide 1]
1. Holy Spirit, gracious guest, Hear and grant our heart's request For that gift supreme and best: Holy heav'nly love.
[Slide 2]
2. Faith that mountains could remove, Tongues of earth or heaven above, Knowledge, all things, empty prove If I have no love.
[Slide 3]
3. Though I as a martyr bleed, Give my goods the poor to feed, All is vain if love I need: Therefore give me love.
[Slide 4]
4. Love is kind and suffers long, Love is pure and thinks no wrong, Love than death itself more strong: Therefore give us love.
[Slide 5]
5. Prophecy will fade away, Melting in the light of day; Love will ever with us stay: Therefore give us love.
[Slide 6]
6. Faith and hope and love we see Joining hand in hand agree— But the greatest of the three, And the best is love.
