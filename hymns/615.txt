[Slide 1]
1. Rise up O men of God! His kingdom tarries long. Bring in the day of brotherhood, And end the night of wrong.
[Slide 2]
2. Let women all rise up! Have done with lesser things. Give heart and soul and mind and strength To serve the King of kings.
[Slide 3]
3. Rise up, courageous youth! The church for you doth wait, Her strength unequal to her task Rise up, and make her great!
[Slide 4]
4. Lift high the cross of Christ! Tread where His feet have trod. Disciples of the Son of man, Rise up, O church of God!
