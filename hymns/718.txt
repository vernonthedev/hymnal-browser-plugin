[Slide 1]
Rejoice in the Lord, O you righteous! for praise from the upright is beautiful. Praise the Lord with the harp; Make melody to Him with an instrument of ten strings.
[Slide 2]
Sing to Him a new song; Play skillfully with a shout of joy. For the word of the Lord is right, And all His work is done in truth. He loves righteousness and justice; The earth is full of the goodness of the Lord.
[Slide 3]
By the word of the Lord the heavens were made, and all the host of them by the breath of His mouth. He gathers the waters of the sea together as a heap; He lays up the deep in storehouses.
[Slide 4]
Let all the earth fear the Lord; Let all the inhabitants of the world stand in awe of Him. For He spoke, and it was done; He commanded, and it stood fast
