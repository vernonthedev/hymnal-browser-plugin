[Slide 1]
Praise the Lord. I will extol the Lord with all my heart in the council of the upright and in the assembly.
[Slide 2]
Great are the works of the Lord; they are pondered by all who delight in them.  Glorious and majestic are his deeds, and his righteousness endures forever.
[Slide 3]
He has caused his wonders to be remembered; the Lord is gracious and compassionate. He provides food for those who fear him; he remembers his covenant forever.
[Slide 4]
He has shown his people the power of his works, giving then the lands of other nations. The works of his hands are faithful and just all his precepts are trustworthy.
[Slide 5]
They are steadfast for ever and ever, done in faithfulness and uprightness. He provided redemption for his people; he ordained his covenant forever-holy and awesome is his name.
[Slide 6]
The fear of the Lord is the beginning of wisdom; all who follow his precepts have good understanding. To him belongs eternal praise.
