[Slide 1]
1. Just as I am, without one plea, But that Thy blood was shed for me And that Thou bid’st me come to Thee, O Lamb of God, I come.
[Slide 2]
2. Just as I am, and waiting not To rid my soul of one dark blot, To Thee whose blood can cleanse each spot, O Lamb of God, I come.
[Slide 3]
3. Just as I am, though tossed about With many a conflict, many a doubt; “Fightings within and fears without,” O Lamb of God, I come.
[Slide 4]
4. Just as I am, poor, wretched, blind, Sight, richness, healing of the mind, Yea all I need, in Thee I find, O Lamb of God, I come.
[Slide 5]
5. Just as I am, Thou wilt receive, Wilt welcome, pardon, cleanse, relieve; Because Thy promise I believe, O Lamb of God, I come.
[Slide 6]
6. Just as I am, Thy love I own Has broken every barrier down; Now, to be Thine, and Thine alone, O Lamb of God, I come.
