[Slide 1]
1. Immortal Love, forever full, Forever flowing free, Forever shared, forever whole, A never ebbing sea!
[Slide 2]
2. We may not climb the heavenly steeps To bring the Lord Christ down; In vain we search the lowest deeps, For Him no depths can drown.
[Slide 3]
3. But warm, sweet, tender, even yet A present help is He; And faith has still its Oliver, And love its Galilee.
[Slide 4]
4. The healing of His seamless dress Is by our beds of pain; We touch Him in life’s throng and press, And we are whole again.
[Slide 5]
4. The healing of His seamless dress Is by our beds of pain; We touch Him in life’s throng and press, And we are whole again.
[Slide 6]
5. Through Him the first fond prayers are said, Our lips of childhood frame; The last low whispers of our dead Are burdened with His name.
[Slide 7]
6. O Lord and Master of us all: Whate’er our name or sign, We owe Thy sway, we hear Thy call, We test our lives by Thine!
