[Slide 1]
When [ Jesus ] was sitting on the Mount of Olives the disciples came and asked him privately, “Tell us, when is this going to happen, and what will be the sign of your coming and of the end of the world?”
[Slide 2]
And Jesus answered them, “Take care that no one deceives you; because many will come using my name and saying, ‘I am the Christ,’ and they will deceive many
[Slide 3]
“You will her of wars and rumours of wars; do not be alarmed, for this is something that must happen, but the end will not be yet.  For nation will fight against nation, and kingdom against kingdom. There will be famines and earthquakes here and there… Then they will hand you over to be tortured and put to death; and you will be hated by all the nations on account of my name.”
[Slide 4]
“This Good News of the kingdom will be proclaimed to the whole world as a witness to all the nations. and then the end will come.”
[Slide 5]
“There will be great distress such as, until now, since the world began, there never has been, nor ever will be again False Christs and false prophets will arise and portents, enough to deceive even the  chosen, if that were possible.  There, I have forewarned you.”
[Slide 6]
“Immediately after the distress of those days the sun will be darkened, the moon will lose its brightness, the stars will fall from the sky and the powers of heaven will be shaken.
[Slide 7]
“And then the sign of the Son of Man will appear in heaven; then too all the peoples of the earth will beat their breasts; and they will see the Son of Man coming on the clouds of heaven with power and great glory.
[Slide 8]
“And he will send his angels with a loud trumpet to gather his chosen from the four winds, from one end of heaven to the other.”
