[Slide 1]
I waited patiently for the Lord; he turned to me and heard my cry. He lifted me out of the slimy pit, out of the mud and mire; he set my feet on a rock and gave me a firm place to stand.
[Slide 2]
He put a new song in my mouth, a hymn of praise to our God. Many will see and fear and put their trust in the Lord.
[Slide 3]
Blessed is the man who makes the Lord his trust, who does not look to the proud, to those who turn aside to false gods.
[Slide 4]
Many, o Lord my God, are the wonders you have done. The things you planned for us no one can recount to you; were I to speak and tell of them, they would be too many to declare.
[Slide 5]
“To do your will, O my God, is my desire; your law is within my heart.” I do not hide your righteousness in my heart; I speak of your faithfulness & salvation. I do not conceal your love and your truth from the great assembly.
[Slide 6]
Do not withhold your mercy from me, O Lord; may your love and your truth always protect me.
