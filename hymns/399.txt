[Slide 1]
1. Beneath the forms of outward rite Thy supper, Lord, is spread In every quiet upper room Where fainting souls are fed.
[Slide 2]
2. The bread is always consecrate Which men divide with men; And every act of brotherhood Repeats Thy feast again.
[Slide 3]
3. The blessed cup is only passed True memory of Thee, When life anew pours out its wine With rich sufficiency.
[Slide 4]
4. O Master, through these symbols shared, Thine own dear self impart, That in our daily life may flame The passion of Thy heart.
