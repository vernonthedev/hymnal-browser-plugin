[Slide 1]
1. O for a heart to praise my God, A heart from sin set free, A heart that always feels Thy blood So freely shed for me.
[Slide 2]
2. A heart resigned, submissive, meek, My dear Redeemer’s throne, Where only Christ is heard to speak, Where Jesus reigns alone.
[Slide 3]
3. A heart in every thought renewed And full of love divine, Perfect and right and pure and good, A copy, Lord, of Thine.
[Slide 4]
4. Thy nature, gracious Lord, impart; Come quickly from above; Write Thy new name upon my heart, Thy new, best name of Love.
