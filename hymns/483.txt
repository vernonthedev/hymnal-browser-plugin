1. I need Thee every hour, Most gracious Lord; No tender voice like Thine Can peace afford. I need Thee, O I need Thee; Every hour I need Thee; O bless me now, my Savior, I come to Thee.
2. I need Thee every hour; Stay Thou near by; Temptations lose their power When Thou art nigh. I need Thee, O I need Thee; Every hour I need Thee; O bless me now, my Savior, I come to Thee.
3. I need Thee every hour, In joy or pain; Come quickly and abide, Or life is vain. I need Thee, O I need Thee; Every hour I need Thee; O bless me now, my Savior, I come to Thee.
4. I need Thee every hour; Teach me Thy will; And Thy rich promises In me fulfil. I need Thee, O I need Thee; Every hour I need Thee; O bless me now, my Savior, I come to Thee.
