1. Sweet Promise is given to all who believe- “Behold I come quickly, Mine own to receive; Hold fast till I come, the danger is great Sleep not as do others; be watchful, and wait.”
Hold fast till I come, sweet promise of heaven The kingdom restored, to you shall be given Come, enter My joy, sit down on My throne Bright crown are in waiting; hold fast till I come.
2. We’ll “watch unto prayer with lamps burning bright; He comes to all others a thief in the night.” We know He is near, but know not the day- As spring shows that summer is not far away
Hold fast till I come, sweet promise of heaven The kingdom restored, to you shall be given Come, enter My joy, sit down on My throne Bright crown are in waiting; hold fast till I come.
3. Yes! This is our hope, tis built on His word- The glorious appearing of Jesus, our Lord; Of promises all, it stands as the sum: Behold I come quickly, hold fast till I come.
