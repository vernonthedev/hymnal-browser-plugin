[Slide 1]
1. O, for a closer walk with God! A calm and heavenly frame, Alight to shine upon the road That leads me to the Lamb.
[Slide 2]
2. Return, O holy Dove! Return, Sweet messenger of rest; I hate the sins that made Thee mourn, And drove Thee from my breast.
[Slide 3]
3. What peaceful hours I once enjoyed! How sweet their memory still! But they have left an aching void The world can never fill. .
[Slide 4]
4. The dearest idol I have known, What e’er that idol be, Help me to tear it from Thy throne, And worship only Thee.
