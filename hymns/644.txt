[Slide 1]
1. O God, whose will is life and good For all of mortal breath: Unite in bonds of servanthood All those who strive with death.
[Slide 2]
2. Make strong their hands and hearts and wills To drive disease afar, To strive against the body’s ills And wage Your healing war.
[Slide 3]
3. By healing of the sick and blind, Christ’s mercy they proclaim, Make known the great physician’s mind, Affirm the Savior’s name.
[Slide 4]
4. Before them set Your gracious will That they, with heart and soul, To You may consecrate their skill And make the sufferer whole.
