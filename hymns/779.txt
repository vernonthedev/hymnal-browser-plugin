[Slide 1]
“Thrones were set in place and one of great age took his seat. His robe was white as snow, the hair of his head as pure as wool. His throne was blaze of flames, its wheels were a burning fire.
[Slide 2]
“A stream of fire poured out, issuing from his presence. A thousand thousand waited on him, ten thousand times ten thousand stood before him.  A court was held and the books were opened.”
[Slide 3]
I saw the dead, both great and small, standing in front of his throne, while the book of life was opened, and other books opened which were the record of what they had done in their lives by which the dead were judged.
[Slide 4]
Then I saw a new heaven and a new earth; the first heaven and the first earth had disappeared now, and there was no longer any sea.
[Slide 5]
I saw the holy city, and the new Jerusalem, coming down from God out of heaven as beautiful as a bride all dressed for her husband.
[Slide 6]
“Here God lives among men. He will make his home among them; they shall be his people, and he will be their God; his name is God-with-them.”
[Slide 7]
“Very soon now, I shall be with you again, bringing the reward to be given to every man according to what he deserves.”
