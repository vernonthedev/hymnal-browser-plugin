[Slide 1]
1. Alas! and did my Savior bleed? And did my Sovereign die? Would He devote that sacred head For someone such as I?
[Slide 2]
At the cross, at the cross where I first saw the light, And the burden of my heart rolled away; It was there by faith I received my sight, And now I am happy all the day!
[Slide 3]
2. Was it for crimes that I have done, He Suffered on the tree? Amazing pity! Grace unknown! And love beyond degree!
[Slide 4]
At the cross, at the cross where I first saw the light, And the burden of my heart rolled away; It was there by faith I received my sight, And now I am happy all the day!
[Slide 5]
3. But drops of grief can ne'er repay The debt of love I owe: Here, Lord, I give myself away; ‘Tis all that I can do!
[Slide 6]
At the cross, at the cross where I first saw the light, And the burden of my heart rolled away; It was there by faith I received my sight, And now I am happy all the day!
