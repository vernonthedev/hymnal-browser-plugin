[Slide 1]
1. Holy Spirit, light divine, Shine upon this heart of mine, Chase the shades of night away, Turn my darkness into day.
[Slide 2]
2. Holy Spirit, power divine, Cleanse this guilty heart of mine; Long has sin, without control, Held dominion o'er my soul.
[Slide 3]
3. Holy Ghost, with joy divine, Cheer this saddened heart of mine, Bid my many woes depart, Heal my wounded, bleeding heart.
[Slide 4]
4. Holy Spirit, all divine, Dwell within this heart of mine, Cast down every idol throne, Reign supreme, and reign alone
