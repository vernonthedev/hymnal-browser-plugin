[Slide 1]
1. I will follow Thee, my Savior, Wheresoe'er my lot may be. Where thou goest I will follow; Yes, my Lord, I'll follow Thee.
[Slide 2]
I will follow Thee, my Savior, Thou didst shed Thy blood for me; And though all men should forsake Thee; By Thy grace I'll follow Thee.
[Slide 3]
2. Though the road be rough & thorny, Trackless as the foaming sea, Thou hast trod this way before me, And I'll gladly follow Thee.
[Slide 4]
I will follow Thee, my Savior, Thou didst shed Thy blood for me; And though all men should forsake Thee; By Thy grace I'll follow Thee.
[Slide 5]
3. Though I meet with tribulations, Sorely tempted though I be; I remember Thou wast tempted, And rejoice to follow Thee.
[Slide 6]
I will follow Thee, my Savior, Thou didst shed Thy blood for me; And though all men should forsake Thee; By Thy grace I'll follow Thee.
[Slide 7]
4. Though Thou leadest me through affliction, Poor, forsaken though I be; Thou wast destitute, afflicted, And I only follow Thee.
[Slide 8]
I will follow Thee, my Savior, Thou didst shed Thy blood for me; And though all men should forsake Thee; By Thy grace I'll follow Thee.
[Slide 9]
5. Though to Jordan's rolling billows, Cold and deep, Thou leadest me, Thou hast crossed the waves before me, And I still will follow Thee.
[Slide 10]
I will follow Thee, my Savior, Thou didst shed Thy blood for me; And though all men should forsake Thee; By Thy grace I'll follow Thee.
