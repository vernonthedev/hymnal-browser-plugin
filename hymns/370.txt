[Slide 1]
1. Christ for the world we sing; The world to Christ we bring With loving zeal; The poor and them that mourn, The faint and overborne, Sin sick and sorrow worn, Whom Christ doth heal.
[Slide 2]
2. Christ for the world we sing; The world to Christ we bring With fervent prayer; The wayward and the lost, By restless passions, Redeemed at countless cost From dark despair.
[Slide 3]
3. Christ for the world we sing; The world to Christ we bring With joyful song; The newborn souls, whose days, Reclaimed from error’s ways, Inspired with hope and praise, To Christ belong.
