[Slide 1]
Oh, sing to the Lord a new song! Sing to the Lord, all the earth. Sing to the Lord, bless His name;
[Slide 2]
Proclaim the good news of His salvation from day to day. Declare His glory among the nations, His wonders among all peoples.
[Slide 3]
For the Lord is great and greatly To be praised; He is to be feared above all gods. For all the gods of the peoples are idols, But the Lord made the heavens.
[Slide 4]
Honor and majesty are before Him; Strength and beauty are in His Sanctuary.
[Slide 5]
Give to the Lord, O kindreds of the peoples, Give to the Lord glory and strength.
[Slide 6]
Give to the Lord the glory due His name; Bring an offering, and come into His courts. Oh, worship the Lord in the beauty of holiness! Tremble before Him, all the earth.
[Slide 7]
Say among the nations, “The Lord reigns; The world also is firmly established, It shall not be moved; He shall judge the peoples righteously.”
[Slide 8]
Let the heavens rejoice, and let the earth be glad; Let the sea roar, and all its fullness; Let the field be joyful, and all that is in it. Then all the trees of the woods will rejoice before the Lord.
[Slide 9]
