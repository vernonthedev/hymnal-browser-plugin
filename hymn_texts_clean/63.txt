[Slide 1]
1. O come, let us sing to the Lord, Come let us every one A joyful noise make to the Rock Of our salvation
[Slide 2]
2. Let us before His presence come With glad and thankful voice; Let us sing psalms of praise to Him, And make a joyful noise.
[Slide 3]
3. For God, a great God and great King, Above all gods, He is; The depths of earth are in His hand, The strength of hills is His.
[Slide 4]
4. To Him the ocean vast belongs, For He the sea did make; The dry land also from His hands, Its form at first did take.
[Slide 5]
5. O come, bow down and worship Him, And kneeling, humbly pray, Come to our Maker and our God, And hear His voice today.
