[Slide 1]
1. Soon shall the trump of God Give out the welcome sound, That shakes death’s silent chamber walls, And breaks the turf sealed ground.
[Slide 2]
2. You dwellers in the dust, Awake, come forth, and sing; Sharp has your frost of winter been, But bright shall be your spring.
[Slide 3]
3. Twas sown in weakness here; ‘Twill then be raised in power; That which was sown in earthly seed Shall rise a heav’nly flower.
