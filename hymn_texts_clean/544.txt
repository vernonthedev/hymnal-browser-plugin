[Slide 1]
1. Jesus, Son of blessed Mary, Once on earth a little child, Pattern fair of holy living, Gracious, loving, undefiled.
[Slide 2]
2. Though Thy eager heart was yearning Heavy laden souls to free, Yet Thou calledst little children In their happiness to Thee.
[Slide 3]
3. Grant that we, like little children, Free from pride and guile my be; Cheerful, trusting, safe, protected By the blessed Trinity.
