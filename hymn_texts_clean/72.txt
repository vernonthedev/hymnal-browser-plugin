[Slide 1]
1. Creator of the stars of night, Thy people’s everlasting light, O Christ, Thou Saviour of us all, We pray Thee, hear us when we call.
[Slide 2]
2. At the great name of Jesus, now All knees must bend, all hearts must bow; And things celestial Thee shall own, And things terrestrial, Lord alone.
[Slide 3]
3. To God the Father, God the Son, And God the Spirit, Three in One, Laud, honor, might, and glory be From age to age eternally. Amen.
