[Slide 1]
1. Christ is made the sure foundation, Christ the head and cornerstone, Chosen of the Lord, and precious, Binding all the Church in one; Holy Zion’s help for ever, And her confidence alone.
[Slide 2]
2. To this temple where we call You, Come, O Lord of hosts, and stay; Come, with all Your loving kindness, Hear Your people as they pray; And Your fullest benediction Shed within these walls today,
[Slide 3]
3. Grant, we pray to all Your faithful All the gifts they ask to fain; What they gain from Your forever With the blessed to retain; And hereafter in Your glory Evermore with You to reign.
[Slide 4]
4. Praise and honor to the Father, Praise and honor to the Son, Praise and honor to the Spirit, Ever three and ever one: One in might and one in  glory While unending ages run!
