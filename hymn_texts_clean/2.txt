[Slide 1]
1. All creatures of our God and King. Lift up your voice with us and sing: Alleluia!  Alleluia! O burning sun with golden beam And silver moon with softer gleam: Oh, praise Him!  Oh, praise Him! Alleluia, Alleluia, Alleluia!
[Slide 2]
2. O rushing wind and breezes soft, O clouds that ride the winds aloft: Oh praise Him! Alleluia! O rising morn, in praise rejoice, O lights of evening, find a voice. Oh, praise Him!  Oh, praise Him! Alleluia, Alleluia, Alleluia!
[Slide 3]
3. O flowing waters, pure and clear, Make music for your Lord to hear, Oh praise Him! Alleluia! O fire so masterful and bright, Providing us with warmth and light, Oh, praise Him!  Oh, praise Him! Alleluia, Alleluia, Alleluia!
[Slide 4]
4. Let all things their Creator bless, And worship Him in humbleness, Oh praise Him! Alleluia! O, praise the Father, praise the Son, And praise the Spirit, three in One! Oh, praise Him!  Oh, praise Him! Alleluia, Alleluia, Alleluia!
