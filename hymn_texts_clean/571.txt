[Slide 1]
1. What does the Lord require for praise and offering? What sacrifice, desire or tribute bid you bring? Do justly; Love mercy; Walk humbly with your God.
[Slide 2]
2. Rulers of men, give ear! Should you not justice show? Will God your pleading hear, while crime and cruelty grow? Do justly; Love mercy; walk humbly with your God.
[Slide 3]
3. How shall our life fulfil God's law hard and high? Let Christ endue our will with grace fortify. Then justly, in mercy we'll Humbly walk with God
