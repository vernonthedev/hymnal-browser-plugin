[Slide 1]
How can you youth remain pure? By behaving as your word prescribes. I have sought you with all my heart, do not let me stray from your commandments.
[Slide 2]
I have treasured your promises In my heart, since I have no wish to sin against you. How blessed are you, Yahweh! Teach me your statutes!
[Slide 3]
With my lips I have repeated them, All these ruling from your own mouth. In the way of your decrees lies my joy, a joy beyond all wealth.
[Slide 4]
I mean to meditate on your precepts and to concentrate on your paths. I find my delight in your statutes, I do not forget your word.
[Slide 5]
Open my eyes: I shall concentrate on the marvels of your Law. Now your word is a lamp to my feet, a light to my path.
[Slide 6]
As your word unfold, it gives light, and the simple understand. Faithfulness is the essence of your word, your righteous rulings hold  good for ever.
[Slide 7]
Universal peace for those who love your Law, no stumbling-blocks for them!
