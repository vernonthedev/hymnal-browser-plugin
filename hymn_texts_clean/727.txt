[Slide 1]
The Lord is my light and my salvation; Whom shall I fear? The Lord is the strength of my life; Of whom shall I be afraid?
[Slide 2]
When the wicked came against me To eat up my flesh, My enemies and foes, They stumbled and fell.
[Slide 3]
Though an army should encamp against me, My heart shall not fear; Though war should rise against me, In this I will be confident.
[Slide 4]
One thing I have desired of the Lord, that will I seek: That I may dwell in the house of the Lord all the days of my life, To behold the beauty of the Lord, And to inquire in His temple.
[Slide 5]
For in the time of trouble He shall hide me in his pavilion; In the secret of His tabernacle He shall hide me; He shall set me high upon a rock
[Slide 6]
And now my head shall be lifted up above my enemies all around me; Therefore I will offer sacrifices of joy in His tabernacle; I will sing, yes, I will sing praises to the Lord.
[Slide 7]
When my father and my mother forsake me, Then the Lord will take care of me.
[Slide 8]
Teach me Your way, O Lord, And lead me in a smooth path, because of my enemies.
[Slide 9]
Wait on the Lord; Be of good courage, And He shall strengthen your heart; Wait, I say, on the Lord!
