[Slide 1]
1. Father, who on us do shower Gifts of plenty from Your dower, To Your people give the power All Your gift to use aright.
[Slide 2]
2. Give pure happiness in leisure Temperance in every pleasure, Wholesome use of earthly treasure Bodies clean and spirits bright.
[Slide 3]
3. Lift from this and every nation All that brings us degradation; Qwell the forces of temptation; Put your enemies to flight.
[Slide 4]
4. Father, You who sought and found us, Son of God, whose love has bound us, Holy Spirit, in us, round us, Hear us, Godhead infinite.
