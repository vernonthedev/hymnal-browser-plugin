[Slide 1]
1. Lord of all good, our gifts we bring You now; Use them Your holy purpose to fulfil. Tokens of love and pledges they shall be That our whole life is offered to your will.
[Slide 2]
2. We give our minds to understand Your ways; Hands, vices, eyes to serve your great design; Hearts with the flame of Your own love ablaze; Thus for Your glory all our pow’rs combine.
[Slide 3]
3. Father, whose bounty all creation shows; Christ by whose willing sacrifice we live; Spirit, from whom all life in fullness flows To You with grateful hearts ourselves we give.
