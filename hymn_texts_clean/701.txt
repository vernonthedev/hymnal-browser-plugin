[Slide 1]
Shout joyfully to the Lord, all the earth. Serve the Lord with gladness; Come before Him with joyful Singing.
[Slide 2]
Know that the Lord Himself is God; It is He who has made us, and not we ourselves; We are His people and the sheep of His pasture
[Slide 3]
Enter His gates with thanksgiving, And His courts with praise. Give thanks to Him; bless His name.
[Slide 4]
For the Lord is good; His loving kindness is everlasting, And His faithfulness to all generations.
