[Slide 1]
1. Jesus invites His saints To meet around His board, And sup in memory of the death And sufferings of their Lord.
[Slide 2]
2. We take the bread and wine As emblems of Thy death; Lord, raise our souls above the sign, To feast on Thee by faith.
[Slide 3]
3. Faith eats the bread of life, And drinks the living wine; It looks beyond this scene of strife Unites us to the Vine.
[Slide 4]
4. Soon shall the night be gone, Our Lord will come again; The marriage supper of the Lamb Will usher in His reign.
