[Slide 1]
1. Give to the winds your fears; In hope be undismayed: God hears your sighs and counts your tears, God shall lift up your head.
[Slide 2]
2. To Him commit your griefs; Your ways put in His hands To His sure truth and tender care Who earth and heave commands.
[Slide 3]
3. O put your trust in God; In duty’s path go on. Walk in His strength with faith and hope, So shall your work be done.
[Slide 4]
4. Leave to His sovereign sway To choose and to command; So you shall, faithful, seek His way How wise, how strong His hand!
