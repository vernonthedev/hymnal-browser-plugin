[Slide 1]
Remember your Creator in the days of  your youth, before the days of trouble come and the years approach when you will say, “I find no pleasure in them.”
[Slide 2]
Even a child is known by his actions, by whether  his conduct is pure and right.
[Slide 3]
How can a young man keep his way pure? By living according to your word. I seek you with all my heart: do not let me stray from your commands.
[Slide 4]
My son, do not forget my teaching, but keep my commands in your heart, for they will prolong your life many years and bring you prosperity.
[Slide 5]
Let love and faithfulness never leave you; bind them around your neck, write them on the table of your heart. Then you will win favor and good name in the sight of God and man.
[Slide 6]
Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on you own understanding; in all your ways acknowledge him, and he will make paths straight.
[Slide 7]
Praise the Lord, O my soul, and forget not all his benefits… He satisfies my desires with good things, so that my youth is renewed like the eagle’s.
