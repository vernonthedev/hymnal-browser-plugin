[Slide 1]
1. Jesus, Your blood and righteousness My beauty are, my glorious dress; Mid flaming world, in these arrayed, With joy shall I lift up my head.
[Slide 2]
2. Bold shall I stand in that great day, Cleansed and redeemed, no debt to pay For by Your cross, absolved I am From sin and guilt, from fear and shame.
[Slide 3]
3. Lord, I believed Your precious blood, Which at the mercy seat of God Pleads for the captives’ liberty, Was also shed in love for me.
[Slide 4]
4. When from the dust of death I rise To claim my mansion in the skies, This then shall be my only plea: Christ Jesus lived and died for me.
