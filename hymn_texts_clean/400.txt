[Slide 1]
1. I come with joy to meet my Lord, Forgiven, loved and free, In awe and wonder to recall His life laid down for me, His life laid down for me.
[Slide 2]
2. I come with Christians far and near To find, as all are fed, Our true community of love In Christ’s communion bread, In Christ’s communion bread.
[Slide 3]
3. As Christ breaks bread for us to share Each proud division ends. That love that made us makes us one, And strangers now are friends, And strangers now are friends.
[Slide 4]
4. And thus with joy we meet our Lord. His presence always near, Is in such friendship better known: We see and praise Him here, We see and praise Him here.
[Slide 5]
5. Together met, together bound, We’ll go our diff’rent ways, And as His people in the world, We’ll live and speak His praise, We’ll live and speak His praise.
