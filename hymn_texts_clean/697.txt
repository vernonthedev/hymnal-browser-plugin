[Slide 1]
Shout with joy to God, all the earth! Sing to the glory of his name; offer him glory and praise!
[Slide 2]
Praise our God, O peoples, let the sound of his praise be heard; He has preserved our lives and kept our feet from slipping
[Slide 3]
For you, O God, tested us; You refined us like silver. You brought us into prison and laid burdens on our backs.
[Slide 4]
You let men ride over our heads; we went through fire and water, but you brought us to a place of abundance.
[Slide 5]
I will come to your temple with burnt offerings and fulfill my vows to you- vows my lips promised and my mouth spoke when I was in trouble.
[Slide 6]
Come and listen, all you who fear God; let me tell you what he has done for me. I cried out to him with my mouth; his praise was on my tongue.
[Slide 7]
If I had cherished sin in my heart, The Lord would not have listened; But God has surely listened and heard my voice in prayer
[Slide 8]
Praise be to God, who has not rejected my prayer Or withheld his love from me!
